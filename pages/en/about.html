Our Mission

At Clear Net Sky, our mission is to create a safer digital world for everyone. We believe that cybersecurity is not just about technology, but about protecting people, businesses, and communities from evolving digital threats.

Founded in 2019, we have grown from a small team of security enthusiasts into a global team from different countries, protecting over 700 civilians in various countries. Our S.O.W.A division (Security Operations Worldwide Alliance) works around the clock to ensure our clients operate safely in an increasingly connected world.
